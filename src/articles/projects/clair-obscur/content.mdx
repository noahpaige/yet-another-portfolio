## The World of Clair Obscur

Each expedition is a requiem, each spell a verse. You are not just a soldier —
you are a story fighting to stay written. Through shifting skies and painted
beasts, the expedition trudges on, one number closer to the end.

### Key Features

- **Turn-based Combat**: Strategic battles with unique painting mechanics
- **Story-driven**: Every choice affects the narrative and world
- **Beautiful Art**: Hand-painted visuals that tell the story
- **Atmospheric**: Haunting soundtrack and immersive world design
